Goals

The orchestra website is intended to increase members'participation by encouraging younger audience(15-30 years) to join the orchestra group. The website is also intended to bring awareness and to encourage people in the community to attend concerts.

Success Evaluation

The orchestra staff already know how many youths participate regularly. The success of the site will be evaluated by counting the number of youths (15-30 years) who attend orchestra group activities and are committed to serving the group. If the number of youths is greater than what they had before the website launch,then the site will be doing its job. Otherwise, some other measures would have to be put in place.

Target Audience

The taget audience in this case is the younger audience such as the instrumental students between the age of 15 and 30 or anyone else interested in orchestra

Instrumental students would need a place to practise and the orchestra group would be ideal for them. Additionally,this group will be very helpful for anyone interested in orchestra, as it offers opportunities to practise and learn from other members
In order to attract the target audience's attention, the importance of playing orchestra will be emphasised, and the appealing motto and images will be used
